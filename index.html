About Me

With over 13 years of experience, I specialize in software engineering, data analysis, and automation, currently contributing as a System Integration & Applications Tool Development Engineer at Valeo Germany. My work focuses on creating high-impact analytical tools and automated solutions for automotive and industrial systems, including the development of a custom-built Measurement Class Library and interactive MATLAB-based interfaces.

My journey spans startups to global automotive leaders. I am passionate about developing software solutions that not only meet technical requirements but also deliver value to clients. Leveraging expertise in C#, Python, and software architecture, aim to create systems that are scalable, efficient, and capable ofhandling complex computational tasks. My approach combines technical expertise with a commitment to quality and continuous improvement, ensuring the delivery of robust and reliable systems. I have consistently delivered impactful solutions across the development lifecycle, from embedded systems to enterprise-grade applications. By building scalable and intuitive tools, my goal is to optimize workflows, enhance data visualization, and support engineering teams in delivering reliable and efficient solutions.
